# Privacy Policy

**Markdown Quick Look** is a local-only macOS application. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What the app does

The app installs a Quick Look extension that renders Markdown (`.md`) files as formatted previews in Finder. It reads the contents of Markdown files you select in Finder — this data never leaves your device.

## Data collection

- **No analytics or telemetry.** The app does not contain any analytics frameworks or tracking code.
- **No network access.** The app makes no network connections of any kind.
- **No accounts or sign-in.** The app does not require or support user accounts.
- **No data sharing.** No data is sent to the developer, to Apple, or to any third party.

## Local preferences

The app stores your preview settings (text size and font choice) in a local App Group container on your Mac. These settings never leave your device and are not backed up to iCloud.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at https://github.com/rkaregaran/MarkdownQuickLook/issues.
